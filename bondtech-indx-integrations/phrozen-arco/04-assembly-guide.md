# Phrozen Arco -- Assembly Guide

Step-by-step installation guide for the INDX tool-changing system on the Phrozen Arco.

> This guide will be populated as the mechanical design is finalized. Currently a template with the expected assembly sequence.

## Prerequisites

- All components from the [BOM](./03-bom.md) and [Common BOM](../common/bom-common.md)
- Stock Phrozen Arco in working condition
- Klipper firmware installed and configured
- Familiarity with the [Mechanical Design](./02-mechanical-design.md) document

## Safety

- Power off and unplug the printer before any mechanical work
- Allow hotend and heated bed to cool completely
- Wear safety glasses when drilling or cutting aluminum
